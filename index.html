/ 01 ABOUT

A generalist who renders the space between concept & craft.

Six years shaping pixels for studios like Nike, Sony Music, Off-White and Framestore. My work sits at the intersection of sculpture, cinema and product — driven by texture, weight and mood.

I move fluidly across Blender, Houdini, Cinema 4D, ZBrush, Substance and Octane — collaborating with directors, art directors and creative technologists to build worlds that feel tactile.
